\section{Panning and Spatialization}


  The opcodes that one can use for panning and spatialization are \emph{hrtfer}
, \emph{locsend}
, \emph{locsig}
, \emph{pan}
, \emph{space}
, \emph{spat3d}
, \emph{spat3di}
, \emph{spat3dt}
, \emph{spdist}
, \emph{spsend}
, \emph{vbap16}
, \emph{vbap16move}
, \emph{vbap4}
, \emph{vbap4move}
, \emph{vbap8}
, \emph{vbap8move}
, \emph{vbaplsinit}
, \emph{vbapz}
, and \emph{vbapzmove}
.
