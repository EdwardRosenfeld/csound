\section{Linear and Exponential Generators}


  The opcodes that generate linear or exponential curves or segments are \emph{adsr}
, \emph{expon}
, \emph{expseg}
, \emph{expsega}
, \emph{expsegr}
, \emph{jspline}
, \emph{line}
, \emph{linseg}
, \emph{linsegr}
, \emph{loopseg}
, \emph{lpshold}
, \emph{madsr}
, \emph{mxadsr}
, \emph{rspline}
, \emph{transeg}
, and \emph{xadsr}
.
